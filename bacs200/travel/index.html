The West Forest

The Forest

Visit our vast and intricate woods on the western peninsula of the continent. Head west and discover the famous Repository of Learning, or head east to Momument Falls and catch the Giddy Nine's last performance.

Sunken Lands

The Sunken Lands

Be wary on your trip through the enourmous swamp known as the Sunken Lands, for creatures of all shapes and sizes lurk there. If you venture deep enough, you may find the Pythoness in her large tree home. Venture deeper, and you may come across the Drowned City, also knows as Darvis Kur.

Whitelake

Whitelake

Whitelake is a great place to go to if you seek to test your endurance! This opaque lake will suck down anything that doesn't strike the lake hard enough, much like oobleck. If you're up for a challenge, you can run across the lake to the island in the center. Don't fall!
